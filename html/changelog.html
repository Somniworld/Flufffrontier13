dutop updated:

  • Added female variants for the polo-and-pants uniforms, thus fixing the invisible-clothes-bug after rolling down.
  • Added rolled-down variants for PTs

23 March 2021

Zulu0009 updated:

  • Added turrets to every docking port and window on the ship looking into space.
  • Added a recharger to the crashed pod.
  • Added mineral processor and console boards to the Bearcat.

15 March 2021

Zulu0009 updated:

  • Several map changes: expanded kitchen, reworked bar, new brig, added ore box to Vox ship, added an inlet to the Gaunt's disposal, changed the hydroponics layout and other small changes here and there.
  • Added a holster belt (hopefully) to the PADV locker, added a smart service revolver to the FT locker for extra cool factor, added plungers around the ship and others.
  • Fixed Byahkee to Byakhee as it should be.

14 March 2021

EvenInDeathIStillServe updated:

  • RIG Gauntlets now cover whatever's in your hand slot when deployed.
